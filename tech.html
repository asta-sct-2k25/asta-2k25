Twins Sparton

Output-Code-Output

×

Twins Sparton - Rules

  • First Round
  • Team size will be two.
  • 30 multiple-choice questions will be given.
  • Every member ought to take part independently.
  • Every participant is required to attend 15 questions.
  • Shortlisted team will be called for second round.
  • Second Round
  • The team member utilizes a computing device to solve the quiz.
  • Every member must have Gmail account.
  • Twenty digital questions are given.
  • Every member takes part independently.

  • Winner will be selected based on the highest score.
  • 10 minutes will be given.
  • Third Round
  • Output of the program will be given.
  • Monitor will be switched off.
  • Participants can type their program using keyboard.
  • Winners can be selected based on output and high programming skill.
