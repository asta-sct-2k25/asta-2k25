CodelQ

Code Debugging

×

CodelQ - Rules

  • First Round
  • The programs will be provided in Python and C.
  • There are twenty multiple-choice questions in the test, and each one is awarded one mark.
  • It is important for participants to arrive at the event location promptly.
  • It is forbidden to use electronic devices within the venue..
  • You are permitted to bring just a pen, and there will be paper available in the hallway.
  • There will be no additional time allotted for the first round, which will last only 20 minutes.
  • The people who made the short list are contacted by phone (20 minutes).
  • Second Round
  • The programs will be given in both Python and C.
  • There are four questions on the question paper, and each one is awarded five marks.
  • It is forbidden to use electronic devices within the venue.
  • Participants are required to modify the provided code and generate the specified output in the system.
  • There will be no more time allotted for the second round; it will last only 20 minutes.
  • The winners are notified via phone calls.

Twins Sparton

Output-Code-Output

×

Twins Sparton - Rules

  • First Round
  • There are just two people on each team.
  • There are 30 multiple-choice questions.
  • Every member ought to take part independently.
  • Every participant is required to attend 15 questions.
  • Teams score an average of 20 points per round.
  • The team advances to the second round if their score is 20.
  • Second Round
  • The team member utilises a computing device to solve the quiz.
  • Every member must have an account with Gmail.
  • Twenty digital questions are given.
  • Every member takes part independently.
  • There are ten questions for each member.
  • Teams score an average of 15 points.
  • Third Round
  • >One team member can view the conditions and output, but their eyes will be closed, and someone else must write the code for that output.
  • An eye-folded member will assist a colleague with accurate output solely through verbal communication they will not accept any actions.
